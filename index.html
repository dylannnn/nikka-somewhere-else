About

Twenty-three years ago, she landed on this planet. She said hello to a whale drinking at the seaside, had a bite of a green strawberry picked up from the ground.

She was born with curiosity.

In the morning, she collected dew in the bucket, and walked around seeking for wild fruits. She started to live with habitants and animals on the island. They taught her how to build tenants, catching fish, etc.
